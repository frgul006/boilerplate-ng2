The markup stuff

Ok, so we're pretty now! Let's start setting up the right markup.

Right now, this is our structure:

-html
--body
---header
---main
----article

Let's make into this:

-html
--body
---header
---main
----article
----nav
----aside
---footer

This is great but try removing some the text in the F12 Debugger so you don't get a scrollbar.

You'll notice that the footer just ends up at wherever the page ends. Let's start by taking care of that and then work our way inwards!
